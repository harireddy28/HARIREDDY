Featured Historical Places

Taj Mahal

A symbol of love built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in the 17th century.

Red Fort

A UNESCO World Heritage site in Delhi, built by Emperor Shah Jahan.

Hawa Mahal

A stunning palace in Jaipur, known for its unique honeycomb windows.

Gateway of India

An iconic Mumbai landmark built during British rule in 1924.

Charminar

A grand monument in Hyderabad, built in 1591 by Sultan Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah.

The tallest brick minaret in the world, built in the 12th century in Delhi.

Victoria Memorial

A grand marble structure in Kolkata, dedicated to Queen Victoria.

Mysore Palace

A royal residence in Karnataka, known for its Indo-Saracenic architecture.

Ellora Caves

A rock-cut cave complex in Maharashtra, showcasing Buddhist, Hindu, and Jain temples.
